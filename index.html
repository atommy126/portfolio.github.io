Welcome!

Hello, and welcome to my portfolio! I'm a game developer with an interest in gameplay mechanics, programming, and agent behavior. I also enjoy narrative work and worldbuilding, both inside and outside of games. I'm currently pursuing a bachelor of science in game design and development at Rochester Institute of Technology, and I'm set to graduate in May 2026.

What I'm up to now:

When I'm not busy with classwork and other life stuff, I've been working on WARD, a dungeon-crawler roguelike. I have most of the basic functionality down, so now I'm focusing on balancing and adding more finalized content.

WARD

Personal Project
WARD is a 2D dungeon-crawler roguelike game currently under development. Your goal is to explore an overrun research facility to close the rift that lays at bottom, all while attempting to survive the anomalies that wait within.

Craft-Up

Academic Project
Craft-Up is a top-down survival-craft game. You must explore the world to find resources that you can use to create tools and weapons. Monsters emerge at nightfall to take your life, so you must be prepared to fight back. As more powerful enemies appear, you gain access to their materials, allowing you to craft powerful weapons and become unstoppable.
