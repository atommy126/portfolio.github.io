About

Hello!

My name is Thomas Anderson, and I'm a game designer who focuses on crafting engaging, enjoyable experiences. I've been designing games for my entire life, from making up games on the playground to daydreaming about potential updates to my favorite games, and now implementing and testing features in my own work. Games have been a large part of my life, and I seek to create games that can give to others what has been given to me.

Background

I am currently a student at Rochester Institute of Technology pursuing a bachelor of science in game design and development. I am slated to graduate in May 2026, and am looking for entry into the professional games industry.

My Favorites

Some of my favorite games include Don't Starve Together, Project Zomboid, The Long Dark, Enter the Gungeon, Baldur's Gate 3, and Portal 2. Outside of video games, I enjoy board and card games, though I've been playing a lot more Magic: The Gathering than anything else lately. Some of my favorite shows include Adventure Time, Gravity Falls, and Frieren.

The Outdoors

When I'm not making or playing games, you can find me hiking or camping. I've enjoyed nature and the outdoors for as long as I can remember. Growing up in scouts, where I eventually earned my Eagle Scout rank, afforded me plenty of opportunities to explore the wilderness and taught me many valuable lessons in teamwork and leadership. Many of my fondest memories are of camping or backpacking trips, and I plan to make many more.
